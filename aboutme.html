ABOUT ME




After a lifetime spent developing my musical skills and playing as a freelance drummer across America, I realized the impact that following your passion has on your life. I also spent two years driving an eighteen wheeler across North America and 12 years training martial arts! I know all of this sounds like it doesnt tie together, but for me it did. I spent most of my life doing what I love and from that learned that if you are able to be yourself, you will gain comfort and confidence and you will see results. During my travels and in my experiences, I met a lot of amazing people that helped shape who I am today. I was challenged to expand my view of the world and realize just how amazing people really are even if they didnt realize it themselves! I believe that technology is capable of bridging these people from various environments and life styles to their desired lives. I've always been passionate about art and design! From creating stained glass art to studying architecture, drawing, woodworking, working with social media promotion/branding, and UX Design.
