Orunje

Orunje provides an online platform that connects people and local healthcare providers in the Chicago area. Patients can electronically request a doctor or nurse practitioner to their homes, offices or hotel rooms. The company already has over 150 licensed physicians and nurse practitioners registered on their website and ready to see patients.

Black Tech Mecca

At Black Tech Mecca, we believe that the development of a thriving black tech ecosystem will empower the black community, propel the tech community, and introduce a new competitive advantage for the city. However, a thriving black tech ecosystem will not just appear out of thin air. It must be deliberately and meticulously constructed.
